FOUNDING KONG LAND

Human coordination is increasingly taking place not in physical locations, but rather digital forums. For the past decade these forums have been hosted, moderated and controlled by interests that are prone to arbitrary censorship and whose existence is in diametric opposition to the privacy of their users. Cryptographic tools like public key cryptography coupled with distributed networks now allow for direct human coordination, free from moderation by centralized institutions. Many thinkers have proposed [May, Szabo] that crypto-organization would be possible at scale given these tools, but only now have we started to collectively realize DAOs as functional governing bodies which collectively hold over $1 billion USD in assets under their control across 65,000 members.

We're now at a tipping point where DAOs are inevitable as the fastest growing means of human coordination in juxtaposition to traditional corporate and state structures, however, they are largely unable to cross the digital to physical barrier. KONG Land is founded on the principle that DAOs can only cross into the physical realm entirely when they are given strong cryptographic guarantees about people, objects and spaces. Our belief is that physical crypto assets enable everyone to participate in these new forms of organization without perfect technical sophistication. KONG Land is intended not just to be a DAO itself, but rather a DAO of DAOs, or digital nation of them, wherein the primary export are physical crypto tools and products and services built upon them.

KONG Cash is the first example of a physical crypto asset that is designed to allow anyone to intuitively earn, spend and save cryptocurrency. It provides a blueprint for a Kong Land DAO focused on stablecoin crypto cash notes in places where inflation in local currencies is high and many people still use cash. KONG Land passports are the first instance of a physical burner cryptoidentity; they can be used not only in cyberspace for on-chain transactions, but also in the real world to gain entry to spaces. SiLo's, a superset of KONG technology are being integrated into merchandise and collectables that have been created and authenticated by projects like MetaFactory and Mattereum. The potential for physical crypto assets is far beyond the scope of what a single DAO could envision, and so KONG is open today to broad coordination.

At inception, KONG Land will export cryptoassets for projects like stablecoins, identities, goods and more. It will provide KONG technology at cost and free from duties to projects that supply a portion of their governance token to the KONG treasury. Given sufficient funds, it will ultimately seek to continue research around more secure forms of physical crypto assets. In some cases the KONG Land will have to interact with tradcorps in order to source and produce materiel, and it will effect entites to achieve those end. Importantly the KONG Land will consider and evaluate key storage devices by their origin and manufacture in order to create an indepedent registry of roots of trust.
